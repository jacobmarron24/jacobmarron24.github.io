#7 Process: Part 2

This time around on my process I continued with my digital theme. However, instead of using actual figures to display my ideas I used basic flats and then played around with inserting different patterns into them. I believe these look a lot cleaner and are more successful. For the presentation I am thinking of combining a bit of the two together, but having more flats and print development work than actual figures.
