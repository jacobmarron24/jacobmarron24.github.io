#1 Experimental Illustration

I am currently working on revamping a collection that means a lot to me, a collection centered around my inspiration and idol, Eminem. This collection never reached its full potential and I am very excited to work on making it the best it can be. Right now I am playing around with my illustration style. The original was just done in marker and does not have that Slim Shady look that I want my illustrations to achieve. The first new one is still done in marker but with pencil shaded skin. The second was all digitally rendered. Which do you think pays the greatest homage to Shady?
