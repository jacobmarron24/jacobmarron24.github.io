#5 New Collection Inspiration

When first thinking of what collection concepts and values that I wanted to put in my portfolio I automatically knew that I wanted them to show the viewer a bit about myself, my life, and what matters the most to me. The Nough Mama (Wendish for grandma) collection is inspired by my childhood and what it was like growing up with a grandparent as a parental figure.

Born in 1919 she is an incredible women (who just celebrated her 98th birthday last weekend) whose heritage stems from those of the nomadic Wendish people.The culture of the Wendish people is next to gone, and it is normally not the part of my heritage that I embrace. This collection embraces the new unexplored parts of my own identity as well as those of the wearer.

By creating a unisex collection for infants and toddlers, this collection strives to become an advocate for the little people of the world exploring their identities to the fullest without the limits that gender identification can hold. While the collection certainly has a way to go, I look forward to exploring this concept more thoroughly in the next few weeks.
