#2 The Collection Design Process

I believe that process is the most important part of designing a collection. It is the part that brings thoughts to paper, that make ideas and concepts into actual physical designs. Here you will find a small glimpse inside my train of thought and where my ideas for the Eminem collection stem from. Throughout my life, Eminem has constantly been a source of inspiration and willpower to keep pushing through, even when I feel like giving up. I admire his story and where he came from and his persistence in doing what he loved until he became the best. This collection is driven by that feeling of transforming nothing into something great that the world can see.
