#6 Nough Mama Process

This collection I have decided to try and do all my process digitally in order for it to match the rest of my portfolios aesthetic. It has been a bit of a challenge to transfer to 100% digital and I am still getting used to putting my ideas onto the computer rather than onto paper.

I have been using the brush tool in illustrator and my drawing tablet thus far. In order to better clarify what is in my head, I plan on using more flat/exploded details as well as samples so everyone else can better understand my process. It has been a difficult start for me to get what I envision of this collection out of my head, attempting to tie this collection to my childhood while making it appropriate for both genders. I still have a ways to go, but I am happy with the direction and spirit the collection is taking.
