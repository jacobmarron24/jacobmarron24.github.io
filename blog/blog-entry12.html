#12 Design Process #2

This past week I have been working on developing a set of characters for my collection. I plan on using these characters as both engineered and repeat prints. The small motif repeat print could also be done in embroidery. The characters I created will be used in a story that I plan to create to go along with the collection and will help kids understand what is happening with our environment especially at the Great Barrier Reef.

I am also creating a story to go with my BFA collection, so I believe using these stories that I write as a continuity in my portfolios would be a good start for next semester. I also worked on some designs for boys, keeping it simple and wearable, with the graphics being the focus.
