#11 The New Design Process

To start off with my design process, I focused on designing around the details that would sell my collection. These details include quilted garments as well as embroidery details, some of which go on top of engineered prints. I also designed a page based on using a neoprene fabric for some silhouettes, which I believe can be tied back to my theme nicely.

As for colors, I am leaning towards the garments being dyed a less bright color from the natural dyes and then having the print on top be more viabrant. This would help show the contrast between what the reef is supposed to look like vs. what it looks like now that it is dying.

I also think white stitching on top of the prints could be a nice touch to represent the bleaching of the reefs. I did some research into charities that a percentage of profits could go towards and I believe that the Australian Marine Conservation Society is perfect. I plan for my next process pages to include embroidery and quilting samples as well as a greater focus on silhouette.
